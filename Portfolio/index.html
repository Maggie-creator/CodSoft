About Me

Malgorzata Mika is a web developer and a digital artist based in Sydney, Australia. She combines her passion for coding and creativity to create stunning websites and digital artworks. She has a bachelor's degree in computer science and a master's degree in fine arts from the University of Sydney. She has worked as a web developer for several companies, including Microsoft, Google, and Shopify. She also runs her own online gallery, where she showcases and sells her original paintings, sculptures, and prints. Jane loves to explore new technologies and techniques, and is always looking for new ways to express herself through her work.
